Why Smart Locker?

Smart Locker is an easy, secure way for customers to collect orders at their convenience and without the queues. This can all be done without adding staff or disrupting retail operations in the process.

Convenience is another advantage that affects the nowadays customers.It allows our client to provide customers with 24 hour satisfaction and service. In the hours where staffing is at a minimum, mainly overnight, our client can depend on our system to act as their “service employees” to provide customers care.
